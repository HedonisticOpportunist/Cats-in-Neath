How to Catify Your Home

Must Have Cat Items

Cats are playful predators that like to explore. As such, they need constant stimulation or they will resort to behaviour that may be undesirable (e.g scratching furniture). Whether you have a Bengal cat or a Persian, it is important to play with your pet in order to establish a healthy relationship. Playing also helps create a routine for your cat that consists of playing/hunting, eating and sleeping.

For this reason, it is important to figure out what kind of activities your cat likes - does it like to climb on shelves or prefer to hide in specific corners? Once you find these things out, you can transform your home into an area that your cat will love!

One important thing to keep in mind is that there is no need to buy expensive items for your cat. Often enough, an old sofa, a cardboard box or even a worn pair of socks will be more than enough to entertain your cat. More often than not, it is better to wait to find out what your cat likes playing with before buying pricey toys. To get you started, here is a list of things you cannot go wrong with:

  • Cat trees - for your cat to climb and use as a scratching post.
  • Cat shelves - let your cat observe you and the household antics from a high vantage point.
  • Toys such as balls or plush mice - let your cat have a toy to kick and bite!
