How to Catify Your Home

Must Have Cat Items

Cats are playful predators that like to explore. As such, they need constant stimulation or they will resort to behaviour that may be undesirable (e.g scratching furniture).

For this reason, it is important to figure out what kind of things your cat likes - does it like to climb on shelves or prefer to hide in specific corners? Once you find these things out, you can transform your home into an area that the cat feels comfortable in.

  • Cat Shelves
  • Toys
