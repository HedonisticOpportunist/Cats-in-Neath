How to Keep Cats off Laptops and Other Issues ...


Common Cat Problems:

The Scenario:

You can probably already picture this. It's Sunday afternoon, and you've finally got enough spare time to read all those horror books that you've been looking forward to. However, the moment you turn on your laptop, here comes kitty and jumps on it! You can no longer do anything but stare at the screen in distress while kitty purrs and falls asleep. Meanwhile, the laptop keeps whirring and whirring ... Let me tell you that there's only one thing to do ... namely nothing! Accept your fate and join the league of people who've found it out the hard way. Cats are evil masterminds who've come to dominate humankind with their cute faces and loud meows!

Forget about Ever Wearing Black Again

Unless you own a Sphynx cat , it's more than likely that your kitty will be shedding fur all throughout the year. This doesn't only mean finding bits of random fur on your leather sofa, but regularly having your clothes covered in cat fur. While it's no biggie if you wear light colours, it's an issue if you're like me and enjoy embracing your Inner Goth. Instead of looking dark and mysterious, you often wind up looking like a cat owner who's long since given up on trying to groom their cat.

Sleep - What's That Mysterious Thing You're Talking About?

Once you become a pet owner, you'll quickly realise one thing: the cat's your boss and you'll do as the boss requires (and not vice versa). If your cat wants attention, it will get it. Even if it's way past midnight and the only thing you want to do is count sheep.
